The problem

Climate change is a problem in the world which can't be avoided anymore. That's why a lot of countries try to contribute by investing in sustainable energy. In Europe agreements are made between countries. However, not all countries are as invested as others. That's why this website gives information about the share of renewable energy in the total energy consumption in the EU.


Visualizations

The overall share per country per year is presented in the map. The overall share per country over the years 2007-2016 is presented in the line graph. The barchart gives the share in three sectors: transport, electricity, and heating and cooling.
